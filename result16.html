Calculate the Area of a Circle, Square, and Rectangle

Explanation:

Write a program to calculate the area of a circle, square, and rectangle based on user input.

Area of a circle = πr²
Area of a square = side²
Area of a rectangle = length × width

Example 1:

Input: shape = "circle", radius = 5
Output: 78.54
Explanation: The area of the circle is π × 5² ≈ 78.54.


Example 2:

Input: shape = "rectangle", length = 4, width = 6
Output: 24
Explanation: The area of the rectangle is 4 × 6 = 24.
