Sum of Squares of n Natural Numbers

Explanation:

Given a number n, calculate the sum of the squares of the first n natural numbers. The formula for the sum of squares is:
Sum = 1² + 2² + 3² + ... + n² = n(n + 1)(2n + 1) / 6
Example 1:

Input: n = 3
Output: 14
Explanation: 1² + 2² + 3² = 1 + 4 + 9 = 14.


Example 2:

Input: n = 5
Output: 55
Explanation: 1² + 2² + 3² + 4² + 5² = 1 + 4 + 9 + 16 + 25 = 55.
