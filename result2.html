Print All Prime Numbers in an Interval

Explanation:

A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and itself. Given two numbers start and end, print all prime numbers in that interval.
Example 1:

Input: start = 10, end = 20
Output: [11, 13, 17, 19]
Explanation: These are the prime numbers between 10 and 20.


Example 2:

Input: start = 1, end = 5
Output: [2, 3, 5]
Explanation: These are the prime numbers between 1 and 5.
